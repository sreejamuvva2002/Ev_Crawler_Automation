Description

Premium, Rail-Served Industrial Site at South Peach Industrial Park

This premier development-ready site is strategically situated within the active and thriving South Peach Industrial Park in Fort Valley, Georgia. It offers a critical advantage for manufacturing and logistics operations with direct rail access, positioning it perfectly for seamless bulk transport and supply chain efficiency.

Key Location & Connectivity

  • Exceptional Highway Access: The park is located directly off the highly-trafficked U.S. Highway 341 (Main Street), providing excellent visibility and immediate connectivity to the major regional transportation network.
  • Proximity to Major Routes: Its location offers convenient access to Interstate 75 (I-75), a vital north-south artery connecting the Southeast, and is within close proximity to Macon, Georgia, enhancing its regional logistical appeal.

Thriving Industrial Community

The South Peach Industrial Park is a proven hub for industry, home to a robust cluster of established national and international manufacturers. This collaborative environment offers potential synergistic opportunities. Current neighbors and key tenants include:

  • Blue Bird Bus Company: A globally recognized leader in school bus manufacturing.
  • Construction Specialties (CS): A multinational manufacturer of architectural building products.
  • ICA South (formerly The Wire Shop): A key player in the industrial components sector.
  • HSM Manufacturing: A diverse manufacturer specializing in components for the furniture, bedding, and transportation industries.

Workforce Development Advantage

The site benefits significantly from its proximity to cutting-edge education resources. The Peach Regional Workforce Development Center, located within the park, is a state-of-the-art facility providing customized training programs and a direct pipeline to a skilled local workforce, ensuring future employers can meet their labor demands efficiently. The Center is a partnership with Central Georgia Technical College (CGTC), further guaranteeing the quality and relevance of its industrial training. It is also the Career Center for Peach County High School.
